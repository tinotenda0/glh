User Guide

Welcome to the GreenField Local Hub! This guide will help you, whether you're a customer looking for fresh local goods or a seller wanting to set up your own digital storefront. [1]

For Customers

As a customer, you can browse stores, purchase products, and participate in our loyalty program. [1]

Getting Started

  • Registration: To get started, you'll need to create a customer account. This automatically enrolls you in our loyalty program! [1, 2]
  • Logging In: Once registered, you can log in to access your account and start shopping. [2]

Shopping

  • Browsing: Explore various stores and the products they offer.
  • Adding to Cart: See something you like? Simply click "Add to Cart" to add it to your shopping basket. [2]
  • Viewing Your Cart: You can view your cart at any time to see the items you've selected, update quantities, or remove items. [2]

Checkout & Payments

  • Checkout: When you're ready to buy, proceed to the checkout page. [2]
  • Applying Coupons: If you have a coupon code, you can apply it in the cart to receive a discount. [1, 2]
  • Using Loyalty Points: You can choose to redeem your loyalty points at checkout for a discount on your order. [1, 2]

Your Account

  • Profile Management: You can edit your profile information at any time. [2]
  • Order History: Keep track of all your past purchases in your order history. [1, 2]
  • Loyalty Program: Visit the loyalty program page to check your points balance and current tier (Bronze, Silver, or Gold). You earn 1 point for every $1 spent, and 10 points can be redeemed for a $1 discount. [1, 2]

For Sellers

As a seller, you can create your own stores, manage products, and track your sales.

Getting Started

  • Registration: To become a seller, you must first have a customer account. Once you are logged in as a customer, you can register to become a seller. This will update your account role. [2]

Store Management

  • Create a Store: Set up one or more digital storefronts using the create store page. [1, 2]
  • My Stores: You can view, edit, or delete your stores from the My Stores page. [2]
  • Store Dashboard: Each store has its own dashboard where you can see its products and orders. [2]

Product Management

  • Adding Products: Add new products to your store, including details like name, description, price, and stock quantity. [1, 2]
  • Managing Products: From your store's dashboard, you can go to "Manage Products" to edit or delete existing products and update their stock levels. [2]

Orders & Coupons

  • Store Orders: Track all orders placed at your store and update their status (e.g., from 'Paid' to 'Shipped'). [1, 2]
  • Coupon Creation: You can create promotional coupons for your customers. These can be percentage-based discounts and can even be restricted to customers in specific loyalty tiers (e.g., a "Gold" tier exclusive coupon). [1, 2]
  • Manage Coupons: View, edit, or delete your store's coupons from the coupon management page. [2]
